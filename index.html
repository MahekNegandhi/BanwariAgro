Durum Wheat Semolina is known for being rich in several essential nutrients. It is highly rich in dietary fibres and proteins.Semolina is the coarse, purified wheat middlings of durum wheat mainly used in making pasta and pizza breads.
Why should you try this ?
Semolina is made from durum wheat, so it is digested slowly. This helps you feel full longer and prevents you from overeating. Semolina improves kidney function, thanks to its potassium content. Add some chopped nuts and raisins to your semolina porridge, and you increase its nutritional content as well as taste.
